About Me

Hi, my name is Richard Verbraak and I'm an aspiring Web Developer from the Netherlands. I got hooked the moment I started learning about Web Development, which was back at the start of February, 2019. I'm 100% focused on Front-End thus far, although I am interested in learning Back-End tech like NodeJS, Express or MongoDB. I am fond of making minimalistic type of designs and creating sleek animations without them being too obtrusive. Outside of all this, I enjoy playing games and watch TV Shows & Movies.

Skills

I have solid knowledge of HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. I primarily use Sass for styling, which makes developing a lot easier with it's nesting, variables and mixins. I also have experience with React.js and I am familiar with libraries like React-Redux and React-Router. For tooling I primarily use NPM and Git. And my go to for writing tests in either JS or React is a testing framework called Jest.
